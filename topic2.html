Dominant Language, Language Family, and Translations

The dominant language spoken in Chicago Chinatown is Mandarin Chinese. Some also speak Cantonese.
The language family of Mandarin is Sino-Tibetan. The language branch of Mandarin is the Sinitic branch, which leads directly to Mandarin as the language.
Common Words Translated into Mandarin:
 - Love- 爱 (Ài)
 - Death - 死亡 (Sǐwáng)
 - Money - 钱 (Qián)
 - Happiness - 幸福 (Xìngfú)
 - School - 学校 (Xuéxiào)
