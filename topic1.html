Location and Maps

The ethnic enclave we chose is located in Chicago, Illinois. It is near Downtown Chicago and is located right next to the Chicago River, Interstate 55, and Interstate 90.

Diffusion, Assimilation, and Acculturation

Things like restaurants, Chinese language, Chinese tradition, and Chinese music diffused into Chinatown in the form of relocation diffusion due to the moving of Chinese immigrants from China in the 18th century.
An example of acculturation is restaurants in Chinatown. They take Chinese food orders in English rather than taking them in Chinese, which shows how the Chinese-Americans are balancing the cultures of America. They are speaking in English to take orders and serving Chinese food.
